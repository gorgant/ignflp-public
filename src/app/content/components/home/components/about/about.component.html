About Us

Greg and Mary Daphne are two avid fitness enthusiasts who wanted a better way to customize and track our online workouts.

They launched Ignyte Fitness to combine and organize their favorite online workout programs and fitness instructors in a single, intuitive interface.

Through Ignyte Fitness, you get streamlined access to the staggering wealth of high-quality fitness content on the web, and join a community of passionate, fitness- obsessed individuals who celebrate the incredible creators behind that content.
